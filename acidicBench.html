Lab Instructions: Acid–Base Titration
Reagents
  • HCl 0.10 M
  • NaOH 0.10 M
  • Phenolphthalein indicator
  • Distilled water for rinsing
Measurements
  • NaOH 25.0 mL
  • Indicator 3 drops
  • Burette start 0.00 mL
  • Endpoint neutral pH 7
Procedure
  1. Put on PPE and collect the burette, flask, funnel, and pipette.
  2. Assemble the stand, clamp, burette, flask, and white tile in order.
  3. Fill the burette to 0.00 mL with HCl.
  4. Transfer 25.0 mL NaOH into the flask and add 3 drops of indicator.
  5. Titrate slowly to the endpoint, record the result, and dispose of waste correctly.

Lab Instructions: Acid–Base Titration
Reagents
  • HCl 0.10 M
  • NaOH 0.10 M
  • Phenolphthalein indicator
  • Distilled water for rinsing
Measurements
  • NaOH 25.0 mL
  • Indicator 3 drops
  • Burette start 0.00 mL
  • Endpoint neutral pH 7
Procedure
  1. Put on PPE and collect the burette, flask, funnel, and pipette.
  2. Assemble the stand, clamp, burette, flask, and white tile in order.
  3. Fill the burette carefully so the liquid rises from the bottom and stop at 0.00 mL.
  4. Transfer 25.0 mL NaOH into the flask and add 3 drops of indicator.
  5. Titrate slowly until the final pink colour disappears and the solution reaches the endpoint.
